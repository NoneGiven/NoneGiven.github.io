Tripcode
Do not include the starting # in your password. Only the first 8 characters of a password will affect the tripcode.

Notes
  • Any ? characters in the "replaced" field are actual question marks, not characters your browser is failing to render.
  • Because of SJIS conversion, entering (for example) ¥ and \ as passwords will generate the same tripcode.
  • However, with HTML entity conversion, entering (for example) & and &amp; will not generate the same tripcode.
