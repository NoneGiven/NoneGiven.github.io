• Enter the number of reps and click a button to begin.
  • Press Space to re-play speech.
  • Press Left and Right to navigate reps.
  • Press R to reset.
"!" Mode:
  • Kana will appear. Say the corresponding kana aloud.
  • Press Space or click to play speech and check your answer.
"?" Mode:
  • Speech will play. Write the corresponding kana by hand.
  • Click the answer box to check your answer.
  • Press Enter to toggle "answer mode" where the kana will be revealed automatically.
"*" Mode:
  • Speech will play. Click the corresponding kana below.
  • Press Enter to toggle the answer buttons between hiragana and katakana.
